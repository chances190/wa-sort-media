Step 2: Backup Contacts

Ensure your contacts are safely backed up to Google

📱 Why backup contacts?

Your WhatsApp contacts are stored on your phone. Backing them up to Google Contacts ensures you never lose them.

Enable Contact Sync on Your Phone

  1. Open Settings on your Android phone
  2. Go to Accounts → Google
  3. Select your Google account
  4. Turn on Contacts sync
  5. Tap Sync now

Access Your Backed-Up Contacts

Once synced, you can access your contacts from any device:
