WhatsApp Media Organizer

Complete workflow to backup, decrypt, and organize your WhatsApp media by contact

📱 What this tool does

  1. Backs up WhatsApp files from your phone via USB
  2. Decrypts the encrypted database using your 64-digit key
  3. Helps you export contacts from Google
  4. Organizes all media into folders by contact name

⚠️ Before you start

  • Have your 64-digit encryption key ready (from WhatsApp Settings → Chats → Encrypted Backup)
  • USB cable to connect your phone
  • About 30-60 minutes depending on data size

Step 1: Backup WhatsApp Files

Connect your phone via USB and backup WhatsApp databases and media

📲 Setup your phone

  1. Connect phone to computer via USB cable
  2. On phone: tap USB notification → select File Transfer (MTP)
  3. Keep phone unlocked during backup

Step 2: Decrypt WhatsApp Database

Unlock your encrypted WhatsApp backup using your 64-digit key

🔐 About the 64-digit key

This is the key you set up in WhatsApp Settings → Chats → Chat backup → End-to-end encrypted backup. If you don't have it, you cannot decrypt the database.

Step 3: Export Contacts (Optional)

Export your contacts for better contact name matching

👥 Why export contacts?

Contacts help match phone numbers to names. Without this, folders will be named by phone number instead of contact names.

Export from Google Contacts

  1. Go to contacts.google.com
  2. Click Export (in the sidebar)
  3. Select vCard (for iOS Contacts) format
  4. Click Export
Skip if you don't have contacts exported

Step 4: Organize Media by Contact

Final step - organize your media into folders by contact

🗂️ What happens next

Your media will be organized into folders by contact name, with subfolders for different media types (Images, Videos, Audio, Documents).
